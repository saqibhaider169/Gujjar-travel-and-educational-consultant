Students Will Need

To successfully apply to universities and secure your student visa, you will typically need to have the following documents and requirements ready.

  • Proof of Enrollment

    Official acceptance/offer letter from your chosen university.

  • Proof of Financial Ability

    To afford the cost of travel, course tuition fees, and living expenses.

  • Valid Six-Month Passport

    Passport must be valid at least six months beyond the length of stay.

  • English Language Proficiency

    Evidence of English proficiency may be required (IELTS/TOEFL/PTE).

  • Medical Report & Health Examination

    A health check-up certificate as required by many destination countries.
