Our Story

Bridging Students & Global Universities

Gujjar Travel and Education Consultant was founded with a single, powerful vision: to remove every barrier that stands between a deserving student and a world-class education. We recognized that the process of studying abroad was unnecessarily complicated, expensive, and opaque — and we decided to change that.

Operating as a dedicated subsidiary of the Gujjar Travel and Tour Group of Companies, we combine decades of travel industry expertise with deep-rooted educational consultancy knowledge to offer a holistic student experience unlike any other.

From your very first consultation to the day you set foot on your chosen campus — and beyond — Gujjar Education is your partner, your guide, and your strongest advocate.

A Word from Our Leader

A Visionary Committed to Student Success

Under the leadership of CEO Chaudhry Moeen, Gujjar Travel and Education Consultant has built a reputation for excellence, integrity, and genuine student-centric service. Chaudhry Moeen's vision was simple yet revolutionary: to create a one-stop platform where students could get expert education guidance and pay their university fees — all without any hidden costs.

"At Gujjar Travel and Tour, our mission is to provide exceptional travel experiences with trust, comfort, and professionalism at every step. The same values are now embedded in our education division. We believe in building long-term relationships through honesty, dedication, and outstanding service. Your journey is our responsibility, and we strive to make every trip — whether it's to a university campus or a pilgrimage destination — memorable and stress-free."

His leadership extends across both the travel and education sectors, creating a powerful synergy that benefits students — from discounted flights to their study destinations to priority university admission services.
