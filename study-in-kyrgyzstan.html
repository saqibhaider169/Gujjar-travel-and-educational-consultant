Why Study In Kyrgyzstan?

Kyrgyzstan has become a prime destination for international students pursuing medicine (MBBS). Universities like the Asian Medical Institute and Kyrgyz State Medical Academy offer WHO and PMC/MCI recognized degrees entirely in English.

Admissions & Cost

The tuition fee for MBBS ranges incredibly low, between $3,000 and $5,000 per year. Living expenses are extremely affordable, typically around $150 to $200 per month, making it ideal for budget-conscious students.
