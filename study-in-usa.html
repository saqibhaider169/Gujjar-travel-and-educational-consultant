Why Study In USA?

Studying in the USA offers high-quality education, a welcoming society, and beautiful landscapes. In addition, the USA also offers excellent post-study work opportunities for graduates. Universities here are world-renowned for their academic excellence and innovative research.

Top Universities In The USA

Harvard University

Harvard is synonymous with excellence. It offers a wide range of programs and strongly focuses on research. Harvard’s alumni network is vast, providing excellent career connections globally.

Stanford University

Located in Silicon Valley, Stanford is a hub for innovation. It offers top programs in engineering, business, and computer science. Its proximity to leading tech companies provides unique internship opportunities.

Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT)

MIT is a leader in science and technology. Known for its rigorous programs, MIT attracts the best minds from around the world. Its research output and innovation are unparalleled.

Admission Requirements in USA

Requirements for admission to US institutions might change based on the school and degree level:

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Standardized test scores (SAT/GRE/GMAT/ACT)
  • Completed application form & fee
  • Statement of Purpose (SOP)
  • Letters of recommendation
  • Professional Resume / CV
  • Portfolio (for creative programs)

Cost Of Studying In USA

Tuition Fees

For overseas students, undergraduate tuition costs typically range from $25,000 to $55,000 a year. Master’s programs range from $22,000 to $50,000 annually. Some top private colleges may charge much more.

Living Expenses

The annual cost of living might vary from $10,000 to $25,000 depending on your city. Usually, housing is the largest portion of your living costs. On-campus housing may be less expensive than private rentals.

Scholarships For International Students In USA

  • Merit Based Scholarships: Offered by several US colleges based on academic excellence.
  • Fulbright Program: Grants for international graduate students to study and conduct research.
  • Aga Khan Foundation: Need-based scholarships for postgraduate studies.

Student Visa Requirements For Students

  • Valid Passport
  • Polio certificate & Medical insurance
  • Visa application fee receipt
  • Passport-sized pictures
  • Genuine Temporary Entrant (GTE) statement
  • Confirmation of Enrolment (eCOE)
  • Financial proof (Tuition, living, & airfare)

Post-Study Work Opportunities

International graduates can stay and work in the United States for a maximum of two to four years with a PSW visa after a Bachelor's degree. Graduates of Master’s programs are eligible for a three- to five-year PSW visa.

These visas enable students to further their careers in the USA, make money, and get helpful job experience at a global scale.
